Bagac is known for its delicious seafood and traditional Filipino cuisine. Many restaurants serve fresh fish, crabs, and shrimp caught from the local waters. One of the must-try dishes is "Sinigang na Hipon," a tangy shrimp soup. Grilled seafood platters and "Adobo sa Dilaw," a turmeric-based adobo, are also popular among visitors.
